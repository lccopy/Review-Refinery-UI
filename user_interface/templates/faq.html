Frequently Asked Questions

How does Review Refinery work?

Review Refinery uses advanced NLP algorithms to search and retrieve scientific articles based on the keywords provided by the user.

Is this service free?

Yes, Review Refinery is completely free for public use.

Where are the data coming from?

The articles are published in PubMed which is a complimentary search platform that primarily taps into the Medline database, offering references and summaries related to life sciences and biomedical subjects.

Why was this project born?

In the vast sea of scientific literature, researchers frequently grapple with pinpointing the articles most pertinent to their area of interest. Recognizing this persistent challenge, we embarked on a mission to devise a sophisticated algorithm. This tool is tailored not just to sift through the literature, but to adeptly zero in on and present the most salient articles, streamlining the systematic review process for scientists.

What languages are supported?

So far, only English language is supported.

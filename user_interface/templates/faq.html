Frequently Asked Questions

How does Article Retriever work?

Article Retriever uses advanced NLP algorithms to search and retrieve scientific articles based on the keywords provided by the user.

Is this service free?

Yes, Article Retriever is completely free for public use.

Where are the data coming from?

All the data are sourced from PubMed.

Why was this project born?

Scientists often struggle with finding relevant articles related to a particular subject. To address this challenge, we decided to build an algorithm that allows them to retrieve the most relevant articles with ease.

What languages are supported?

So far, only English language is supported.
